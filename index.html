Thai Mango Sticky Rice Recipe

Mango sticky rice is a popular Thai dessert made from sweet sticky rice, fresh mango slices, and a rich coconut milk sauce. It is known for its delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors, often garnished with toasted sesame seeds or crispy mung beans.

Ingredients

Coconut Sticky Rice

  • 1 cup white glutinous rice
  • ⅔ cup coconut milk
  • ½ teaspoon table salt
  • ½ cup sugar

Salted Coconut Sauce

  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • ¼ teaspoon table salt
  • 1 teaspoon rice flour
  • 1 tablespoon water

Other

  • 3 ripe mangoes

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the rice for 4 hours.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. While the rice is cooking, make the coconut syrup:
    • Combine ingredients in a small pot. Cook over medium heat until it starts to bubble. Turn off the heat and cover.
    • A few minutes before the rice is done, reheat the syrup until steaming hot. When the rice is done, transfer it to a mixing bowl and pour the syrup over. Stir, cover, and let sit for 20 minutes.
  4. Make the salted coconut sauce:
    • Mix the rice flour and water until smooth.
    • Combine the slurry with coconut milk and salt in a small pot. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring constantly until it boils. Remove from heat and let cool.
  5. Assembly: Serve the sticky rice beside fresh mango slices. Drizzle with salted coconut sauce and garnish as desired.
